My research interests

Fast Radio Bursts

  • Time series analysis
    (Spin period search, waiting time, multi-peak component)
  • Polarization characteristic
    (Orthogonal jump)

Pulsar Search

I am working on searching and verifying new pulsars using FAST.

I found 47 new pulsars in Commensal Radio Astronomy FAST Survey (CRAFTS)

Pulsar Birth

I'm working on the radiation geometry of pulsars through polarization observations and studying the spin-kick alignment of pulsars when they birth.

Image source: https://www.mpifr-bonn.mpg.de/research

I am interested in the origin of fast radio bursts and the connection between FRBS and pulsars.
